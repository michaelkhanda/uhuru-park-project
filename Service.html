OUR SERVICES

"An exciting park located at the heart of the touristic city of Nairobi. It is famously known for its modern relaxation amenities which are designed to suit every demographic segment of the society. It boast of clean water bodies which host several recreational activities. It is very accessible and is close to several classic hotels to host to tourists."

Nature Trails

It provides an opportunity to explore and learn about the environment, with informational signs or markers along the way. The trail may include various viewpoints. It encourages physical activity, education, and appreciation of the park.

Jogging Tracks

An outdoor jogging trail is a path or route designed for running, typically located in a natural setting such as a park or forest. It provides a scenic and stimulating environment for aerobic exercise and can range in distance and terrain, from easy to challenging.

Uhuru Lake

Uhuru Lake provides a peaceful and relaxing environment for outdoor activities, like boating, or fishing. Its serene and picturesque surroundings make it a popular destination for tourists and nature lovers.


Outdoor Library

The library provides a unique and refreshing way to read, study, or work outside in the fresh air, and encourages a sense of community by bringing people together in a shared public space. It also includes comfortable seating, shade, and other amenities for a pleasant outdoor experience.

Outdoor Gym

The Gym provides a free and accessible fitness experience to the community, with exercise equipment designed for outdoor use, such as pull-up bars, resistance machines, and cardio equipment. It encourages a healthy and active lifestyle by providing a convenient and affordable option for exercise, and can be used alone or in groups for a fun and challenging workout in the fresh air.

Kids Playground

It provides a safe and stimulating environment for children to exercise, socialize, and learn, with various play structures, equipment, and activities, such as slides, swings, climbing structures, and sandboxes. It encourages children to use their imagination, build physical strength and coordination, and develop social skills by interacting with other children.
