About Us

Welcome to Uhuru Park! Our park is located in the heart of the city and offers a range of activities for all ages. Whether you're looking for a place to exercise, relax, or spend time with your family and friends, we have something for everyone.

Our park features several playgrounds, picnic areas, walking trails, and sports fields. We also have a lake where you can rent paddle boats and enjoy the scenery.

We take great pride in our park and strive to maintain a clean and safe environment for all visitors. Our staff is always available to answer any questions you may have and ensure that your visit is a pleasant one.

Thank you for considering Uhuru Park as your destination for outdoor fun and relaxation. We look forward to seeing you soon!
